SIX-MONTH COURSE

SEWING

Sewing skills are invaluable for personal use, small businesses, and the fashion industry. Mastering sewing techniques enables individuals to create or repair garments, reducing costs and waste. It also provides opportunities for entrepreneurship and sustainable living through customized clothing or tailoring services.

CONTENT

Fees: R1500
Purpose: To provide alterations and new garment tailoring services

  • Types of stitches
  • Threading a sewing machine
  • Sewing buttons, zips, hems, and seams
  • Alterations
  • Designing and sewing new garments

opportunities

There is a demand for tailors, and our sewing course equips students with the tools to get a foot in the door.
Successfully completing the course qualifies you to be a:

  • Tailor
  • Seamstress
  • Alterations manager
