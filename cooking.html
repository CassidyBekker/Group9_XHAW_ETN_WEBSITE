SIX-MONTH COURSE

COOKING

Cooking is a vital life skill that ensures self-sufficiency, nutrition, and cost-effective meal preparation. Knowledge of cooking techniques allows individuals to make healthier food choices and reduce reliance on processed or fast food. It also offers entrepreneurial opportunities in catering or home-based food businesses.

CONTENT

Fees: R750
Purpose: To prepare and cook nutritious family meals

  • Nutritional requirements for a healthy body
  • Types of protein, carbohydrates, and vegetables
  • Planning meals
  • Preparation and cooking of meals

Opportunities

This course takes you on a culinary journey to greatness, providing skills that can be utilized in the kitchen and the job.
Successfully completing the course qualifies you to be a:

  • Food Stylist
  • Caterer
  • Host of a Cooking Studio
