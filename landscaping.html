SIX-MONTH COURSE

LANDSCAPING

Landscaping combines creativity and environmental knowledge to design aesthetically pleasing and sustainable outdoor spaces. It improves the value and functionality of properties, from homes to commercial spaces. Understanding landscaping techniques also contributes to eco-friendly practices, such as water conservation and the use of native plants.

CONTENT

Fees: R1500
Purpose: To provide landscaping services for new and established gardens

  • Indigenous and exotic plants and trees
  • Fixed structures (fountains, statues, benches, tables, built-in braai)
  • Balancing of plants and trees in a garden
  • Aesthetics of plant shapes and colours
  • Garden layout

opportunities

There is a growing demand for garden maintenance, as more people move into homes with gardens but lack the time and knowledge to care for and maintain them.

Successfully completing the course qualifies you to be a:

  • Lawn care specialist
  • Groundskeeper
  • Landscape Architect
