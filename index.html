ABOUT US

Established in 2018, Empowering the Nation offers an innovative training program specializing particularly on enhancing the skills of domestic workers and gardeners, to increase their employability. We offer a large range of training courses, providing the community with the opportunity to chooses between six-week short skills training programmes or six-month long learnerships. With a 95% success rate and recognition as the 15th most valuable skills training institution in Gauteng, we ensure that students who complete our programs not only gain practical, marketable skills but are also well-prepared to secure well-paying employment or successfully launch their own businesses.

6-Week Courses

Our college is dedicated to giving you real-world, hands-on training so you may be successful in the job. Our carefully planned six-week courses are aimed at equipping you with useful skills in areas such as gardening, cooking, and childcare. We offer the ideal course for you, regardless of your goals—whether they want to launch a new profession or expand your current skill set. Are you prepared to change the course of your future?

6-Month Courses

The six-month courses offered by our institution are structured educational programs designed to provide participants with in-depth knowledge and practical skills in specific areas like First Aid, Sewing, Landscaping, and Life Skills. Over six months, students engage in theoretical and hands-on learning, allowing them to develop expertise and become more competitive in the job market. The extended duration ensures that participants have sufficient time to fully grasp and apply the skills they learn.
